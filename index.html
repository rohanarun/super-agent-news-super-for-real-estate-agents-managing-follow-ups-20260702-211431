Why agentic AI matters in real estate now

Speed‑to‑lead is everything

Research shows agents who respond within minutes are dramatically more likely to connect, yet average response times stretch to days. Modern AI assistants are built to answer instantly, qualify, and route leads without waiting for you to click anything [arahi.ai].

From chatbots to agents

The market is moving from text‑only chatbots to agentic AI — systems that can take action in software, maintain state, and complete multi‑step workflows [mit.edu].

Computer use is now mainstream

Google made computer use a first‑class feature in Gemini 3.5 Flash, validating that real browser and app control is essential for serious automation [blog.google], [memeburn.com].

Security and realism matter

As agents gain power, poorly designed automation can introduce real risk. Thoughtful computer‑use design and scoped execution are critical [scmedia.com], [darkreading.com].
